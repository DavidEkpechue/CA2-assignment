Regular Expression Answers
===========================

1. Any open tag without whitespace around the element name and without attributes (e.g. <div>)
   Regex: <[a-zA-Z][a-zA-Z0-9]*>
   

2. Any close tag not containing whitespace (e.g. </div>)
   Regex: </[a-zA-Z][a-zA-Z0-9]*>
   

3. Any tag with any contents (name, attributes, whitespace)
   Regex: </?[^>]+>
   

4. A valid JavaScript variable name in terms of constituent characters and their ordering
   Regex: [a-zA-Z_$][a-zA-Z0-9_$]*
